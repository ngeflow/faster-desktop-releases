macOS: first-time open

The app isn't notarized yet, so macOS Gatekeeper will refuse to open it. Two ways past it:

  • Right-click the app in Applications → Open → confirm.
  • Or remove the quarantine flag in Terminal:
xattr -cr "/Applications/Ngeshare Desktop.app"
System requirements
  • macOS: 11 Big Sur or later, Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3/M4). Intel Macs not supported in v0.1.0.
  • Windows: Windows 10 or later, 64-bit.
